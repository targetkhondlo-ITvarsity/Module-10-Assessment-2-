Cookies

Prep time: 30 Mins

Yield: 1 ½

Ingredients











Steps

  1. Stir together butter and Parmesan cheese in a small bowl.
  2. Spread 1 ½ tsp. butter mixture on 1 side of each bread slice. Place 4 bread slices, buttered sides down, on wax paper. Top with provolone and mozzarella cheeses; top with remaining bread slices, buttered sides up.
  3. Cook sandwiches, in batches, on a hot griddle or in a non-stick skillet over medium heat, gently pressing with a spatula, 4 minutes on each side or until golden brown and cheese is melted.
